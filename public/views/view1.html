FIND AND BE FOUND


HYPEnings allows users to create digital flyers to a one mile radius of wherever they're posted. Other users can then find these flyers with the app upon entering its radius.

EXPLORE YOUR CITY


This symbiotic app encourages both local vendors to digitally promote fleeting deals wherever the want and users to find them by exploring their surroundings. But you gotta move! Flyers disappear 48 hours after posting.

GET OUT THERE!


Whether you're a restaurant promoting a limited time menu, a retail store pushing a BOGO sale, or a local artist advertising an upcoming show, HYPEnings is the perfect bridge between your message and curious travelers.
